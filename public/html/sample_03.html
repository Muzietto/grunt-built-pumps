grunt-built-pumps sample_03

This page features a row of bidirectional pumps. Each pump has an indicated flow rate and maintains the level of the basin above between the values indicated by the positional probes (the sensors) in the water.
When water must be carried away from the basin, it gets pumped in the basin on the 
right
(if any). When water must be pumped into the basin, it is taken from the basin on the 
left
(if any).
Unfortunately, it is possible for a pump to get running just for a flicker, without you being able to notice it turn green.
The direction left/right of the arrow inside the pump indicates which way the water is being pushed.
