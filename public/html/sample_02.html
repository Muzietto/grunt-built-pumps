grunt-built-pumps sample_02

Among the objects in this page 
var flowConn
represents the flow of liquid inside the first diagonal pipe on the left. Its API allows to turn it off and on using respectively 
switchOff()
and 
switchOn()
Click the 
flowConn.switchOff()
button and watch the pump on the left restore automatically an acceptable water level (if the left sensor says it's necessary).
Click the 
flowConn.swithOn()
button and the pipe will start again draining water from the left basin.
The pump on the right starts (turns green) if there is too little water in the rightmost basin basin, taking it away from the one on its left.
Pumps keep running as long as their sensors request it, even if there is no water to take from their source basin. Watch out that flow rates are relative numbers.
flow0
(initially closed) is the diagonal pipe on the right side of the page. By acting on the four buttons you can have water flow through the whole system.
