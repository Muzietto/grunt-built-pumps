Among the objects in this page 
var evlevello
represents the level of the white liquid in the basin. Its API allows to manipulate its 
value()
Type 
evlevello.incr(20)
in the console and watch the pump on the left restore automatically an acceptable water level.
Type 
evlevello.decr(20)
in the console and watch the pump on the right restore automatically an acceptable water level.
